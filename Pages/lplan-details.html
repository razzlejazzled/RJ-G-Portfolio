Details

This MERN Application was made by a team of 4 over the course of 1.5 weeks as the final project for their bootcamp. L-Plan allows teachers to create lessonplans, and share them with other teachers, allowing for greater collaboration. For example, two science teachers in the same building or district who teach different sections of the same class will be able to share their lesson plans for that class. As a MERN App, the front end is based in React.JS, while the back end is based in MongoDB and Express. Other Technologies include Bulma CSS, Mongoose, React-Router, Moment, and Path.

As a general rule, the team of 4 was divided into two sub-teams. R.J., along with a peer, was responsible for the Front End of the site, that is to say they were responsible for React. However, at times the team would all come together to tackle certain tasks. Thus, while in most of Mr. Gassner's work was on the React side of things, he contributed to the Express and MongoDB code as well.
